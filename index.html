Arc 1: The Introductory Arc (September 2017 - June 2018)

All great anime must start somewhere, and our anime is no exception. Dating back to September of 2017, the first few weeks of Grade 9 at a highschool most of us didn't know and in a program we didn't know what would entail. I had incredible luck and became a friend of yours quite quickly, as we began to sit beside eachother in Math Class, and then meeting the rest of the Boiz™ slowly after that. Grade 9 didn't include our most favourite moments, and it wasn't nearly as active as all the other arcs, but we have all got to start somewhere, no? Arc 4: Junior Year (September 2019 - March 2020)

This arc wasn't too eventful, but it was still incredibly fun, I guess you could consider it a filler arc? Grade 11 was tough, but we did what we could to get through it. It sucked a bit because all of the Boiz™ were in separate classes both semesters, and each of us had our own set of responsibilities to attend to, but it made the lunch times together and the occasional walks together that much more valuable (we could talk all about the wonderful experiences we had with our english teachers). It was cut short unforunately due to the global pandemic, but the time we were able to spend together was time well spent. The highlight of this semester was probably going to your house, playing smash, eating pizza and just chilling (unforunately without Josiah), and losing my Presto card in your couch.

Arc 5: The Discord and Google Meets Arc (March 2020 - Present)

This arc is pretty self-explanatory, once the global pandemic hit, everything went virtual, including the Boiz™. Discord suddenly became our meeting place, and it has remained that way since. All was well, until the Google Meets Nation decided to attack and hit us with online courses, which was annoying, but didn't stop us from vibing the entire time. It's still going on to this day and even though it's not the same as physically seeing all of my friends, it is still great that I am able to interact with y'all on a daily basis. Nothing can stop the Boiz™ from being the Boiz™, not even a raging global pandemic.
